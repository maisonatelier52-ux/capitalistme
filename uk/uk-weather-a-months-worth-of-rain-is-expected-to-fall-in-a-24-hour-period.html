UK weather: A "month's worth of rain" is expected to fall in a 24-hour period

By LUCAS HARRINGTON

December 1, 2025

People in the UK are getting ready for bad weather because weather experts say it could rain for a month in just one day. The heavy rain will definitely cause a lot of problems, especially in Wales, where the ground is already wet and the hills are steep, which makes landslides more likely.

The British Geological Survey (BGS) has said that "deep floodwater is possible" in a number of places. Experts say that the amount and strength of the rain that is expected could be too much for drainage systems to handle.This would cause floods to happen more often.

The BGS also says that Wales is in a lot of danger right now because it has been raining a lot for a long time, which has already made the soil loose and the slopes unstable.The rain that is expected could cause more landslides, which would hurt homes, roads, and other infrastructure.

People who live in areas with a lot of risk are being told to stay alert, pay attention to what their local leaders say, and be ready to leave if things get worse.

A big flood threat for the whole UK

Meteorologists think that the sudden rise in rain could cause huge floods, rivers to overflow, and make it hard to get around. Emergency services may have to work harder when it rains or the water level goes up.

Watching over things and making sure the public is safe

As the weather system changes, officials are always watching it and getting ready to move quickly.The Environment Agency and local governments have also told neighborhoods that are in danger.

As the storm gets closer, people should check on their property, keep an eye on the weather, and make sure they are safe.
