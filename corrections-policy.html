Corrections Policy

Accuracy is the foundation of Capitalistme's journalism. While we verify every story carefully before publication, mistakes can still occur. When they do, we correct them openly, quickly, and visibly.

How We Handle Mistakes

Minor errors: Spelling, grammar, or typographical issues that do not affect meaning are fixed immediately without a separate correction notice.

Factual errors: Mistakes involving names, statistics, or verifiable facts are corrected within the article. A clearly marked editor's note explains what was changed and why.

Developing stories: As news events unfold, articles may be updated with new information. All updates include time stamps.

Where Corrections Appear

All corrections are made directly on the affected article page. We never hide corrections or move them to separate locations.

What a Correction Request Should Include

  • The article URL or headline
  • The specific line or claim believed to be incorrect
  • The factual basis for the objection
  • Supporting documentation if available

Reader Submissions

If you notice an error, please contact our editorial team at corrections@capitalistme.com. Our team reviews all requests promptly.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We never remove errors without acknowledgment. Significant changes are always disclosed clearly to readers.

Why This Matters

Trust is built through accountability. By acknowledging our mistakes openly, we aim to provide journalism that readers can rely on.
