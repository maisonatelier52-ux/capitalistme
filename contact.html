Get in Touch

Capitalistme is an independent digital publication. We welcome tips, corrections, and dialogue from readers, journalists, organisations, and anyone referenced in our coverage.

Editorial & General Contact

Questions about our reporting, story suggestions, or general editorial matters can be sent to:

Email: editorial@capitalistme.com

Confidential News Tips

If you have information you believe should be investigated or reported, please share it with our newsroom. We take confidentiality seriously and will handle all submissions with care.

Secure Email: tips@capitalistme.com

Corrections

If you believe we have made a factual error in any of our articles, please inform us so we can promptly correct it. Include the article URL and a clear description of the issue.

Corrections Email: corrections@capitalistme.com

Media & Press Inquiries

Journalists, researchers, or organisations seeking collaboration, interviews, or additional information should contact our media relations team.

Media Email: media@capitalistme.com

Permissions & Legal

For reprint requests, syndication inquiries, or legal concerns, please contact our permissions department.

Legal & Permissions Email: legal@capitalistme.com

When reporting an error, challenging a factual claim, requesting a reply, or sending a permissions or legal concern, please include the article URL and the specific issue so the request can be routed quickly.
