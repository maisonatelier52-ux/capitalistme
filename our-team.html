Our Team

Capitalistme is built by a distributed team of financial journalists, editors, and analysts based across the United Kingdom. We are united by a commitment to independent, fact‑based reporting.

Lucas Harrington
UK Journalist
Lucas Harrington writes short, engaging articles about national and regional news, government, public policy, and social issues. His reporting is clear, honest, and dedicated to the truth.
Amelia Redford
Political Journalist
Amelia Redford covers Westminster, government policy, party dynamics, and elections. She has a degree in international relations and delivers fair, thorough reporting on complex political events.
Daniel Whitmore
Investigations Journalist
Daniel Whitmore publishes long‑form pieces on government accountability, corporate misdeeds, and public‑interest investigations. He excels at exposing systemic weaknesses and holding power to account.
Ethan Calderwood
Sports Journalist
Ethan Calderwood delivers lively coverage of football, rugby, and emerging athletes. His sharp game analysis, athlete interviews, and storytelling capture the excitement of UK sports.
Isabelle Crowley
Technology Journalist
Isabelle Crowley covers digital culture, tech startups, and cybersecurity. With a computer science background, she makes complex tech topics accessible and insightful.
Marcus Ellington
Business Journalist
Marcus Ellington writes about market trends, economic policy, and company strategy. His finance background gives his work a clear, data‑driven perspective, turning financial news into engaging stories.
Oliver Marston
Culture Journalist
Oliver Marston covers art, music, books, and contemporary cultural movements. His insightful storytelling captures the spirit of modern culture in the UK and beyond.
Sofia Trentham
World News Journalist
Sofia Trentham covers geopolitical trends, global issues, and diplomacy. Having reported from Europe, Asia, and the Middle East, she brings nuanced analysis to international events.
