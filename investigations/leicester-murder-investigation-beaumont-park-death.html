Man Arrested After Death in Beaumont Park: Leicester Murder Investigation

By DANIEL WHITMORE

April 7, 2026

People in Leicester are quite upset after hearing that Leicestershire Police are looking into a murder after a 62-year-old man was found unconscious in Beaumont Park and died.The event, which brought a lot of emergency services to the Beaumont Leys neighborhood on Monday night, has now turned into a full criminal investigation as investigators try to figure out what really happened in the busy public place.

Sudden Find in Beaumont Leys Park

The alarm went out for the first time just before 7 p.m. on April 6, when the East Midlands Ambulance Service was summoned to reports of a guy in the parkland behind the Tesco Extra store who was in danger of dying.When paramedics got there, they found the victim and gave him quick medical care before taking him to a local hospital.Sadly, the man died from his injuries a short time later.There are still a lot of police officers at the scene today, April 7, 2026, as detectives try to figure out what happened that led to this terrible conclusion.

An arrest was made as the investigation got more serious

Police have stated that a 33-year-old man is in custody on suspicion of murder, which is a big step forward in the investigation into the murder in Leicester.After early investigations, the suspect was arrested and is still being questioned by professional authorities while being held.Detective Inspector Kevin Hames of the East Midlands Murder inquiry Team said that the inquiry is still in its early stages, even though an arrest has been made.He stressed that the victim's family has been found and is being helped by professional liaison officers at this difficult period.

Forensic Investigations and Public Appeals

There is a wide cordon between Krefeld Way and Beaumont Way so that forensic teams can carefully go over the area where the victim was found.Leicestershire Police are doing a lot of door-to-door investigations and looking at local CCTV material to see what happened in and around the park on the night of the crime.Detectives need this search for evidence to put together what happened before the 62-year-old man's death.

Call for Community Support and Witnesses

The police are now asking anyone who may have been near Beaumont Park on Monday night to come forward with any information, no matter how trivial it may seem.They really want to talk to anyone who may have seen a fight or spotted strange conduct around the park exits.People who were driving in the neighborhood at the time are also asked to look at their dashcam footage.Anyone who knows anything about this can call the police at 101 or use their online portal and mention incident 572 from April 6, 2026.
