Oldham is Greater Manchester's Town of Culture from 2026 to 2027

By OLIVER MARSTON

March 30, 2026

Oldham was named Greater Manchester Town of Culture 2026–27, which is a big deal for the area.This coveted title, which was confirmed on March 27, 2026, marks the start of a new era for the borough as it gets ready to take center stage in the city-region's cultural calendar.The choice shows that the neighborhood has a long history of industry and is dedicated to creating a creative space that is welcoming to everyone.Both local leaders and inhabitants are quite proud of the announcement.They see it as a great chance to show off the town's unique spirit to a larger audience across the UK.

Oldham Wins the Famous Town of Culture Award

The excitement in the borough is clear today that Oldham has won the prestigious Town of Culture title after a tough selection process.This award is more than simply a badge of honor; it also helps the economy and society get back on their feet.The town is ready to improve its local identity and get more people involved in civic life by becoming the Greater Manchester Town of Culture 2026–27.The artists, community groups, and volunteers who have worked hard to show that the town is full of creativity and strength deserve this win.

Getting a £50,000 Grant for Local Innovation

Financial help is a big part of this success story.The town was able to get a £50,000 grant to pay for its big plans.The Greater Manchester Combined Authority is putting money into this project to make it easier for local creators to get started and to make sure that all citizens can enjoy high-quality cultural events.The money provides a base for a long-term growth plan, which lets the borough invest in its infrastructure and give resources to little projects that might not get enough attention otherwise.This money will make sure that the future celebrations are both memorable and meaningful.

Starting the Year-Long Rising Up Program

The year-long "Rising Up" program is at the center of the celebrations.It is a carefully planned sequence of events meant to inspire and engage.This project wants to tell the tale of the town's past while also looking ahead to a bright and creative future.The "Rising Up" motif really speaks to the community since it represents a group rise and a fresh sense of aspiration.The program will connect several neighborhoods over the 2026–27 season, encouraging individuals to look at their own neighborhoods in new and imaginative ways.

A Busy Schedule of Art and Festivals

An full program of festivals and art will change the local scene by bringing color and excitement to the streets.There is something for everyone to enjoy, from big outdoor works to small gallery shows.These art initiatives and festivals are meant to get local schools and youth groups involved, so that the future generation of talent may have their say.The community will become a living gallery while the year-long "Rising Up" program goes on.This will show that art is not just something to look at in a museum, but an important aspect of everyday life.
