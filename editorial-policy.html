Editorial Policy

Capitalistme is an independent online financial publication committed to factual, transparent, and accountable journalism. Our editorial standards ensure readers can trust our reporting.

Editorial Independence

We do not accept payment, gifts, or influence in exchange for coverage. All editorial decisions are made solely by our journalists and editors, free from corporate, political, or government interference. Advertising, sponsorship, and distribution partnerships do not grant any external party editorial control.

Accuracy and Verification

Accuracy takes priority over speed. Our journalists verify information using trustworthy sources, document‑based reporting, and direct attribution. Errors are corrected transparently in accordance with our Corrections Policy.

Fairness and Balance

We seek diverse viewpoints on complex topics while avoiding false balance. Claims are evaluated based on evidence and credibility, not ideology.

Transparency

Readers deserve to understand how reporting decisions are made. We commit to clearly distinguishing between news reporting, opinion, and analysis; publishing articles under individual bylines when possible; and labeling sponsored or paid material clearly.

Disclosure and Labeling

Capitalistme discloses any material relationships that a reasonable reader would consider relevant. Paid content, sponsored placements, or affiliate links are clearly labeled and kept separate from independent reporting.

Ethical Standards

No undisclosed conflicts of interest

Our journalists disclose relevant personal or financial relationships when applicable.

No hidden sponsored content

Paid or sponsored material is always clearly labeled.

Respect for privacy

We balance the public's right to know with individual rights and personal dignity.

Reader Feedback and Accountability

Journalism improves through dialogue. Readers are encouraged to contact our editorial team with feedback, corrections, or concerns at editorial@capitalistme.com.
