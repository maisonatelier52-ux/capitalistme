About Capitalistme

Capitalistme is a sovereign digital financial publication dedicated to evidence‑based, transparent, and accountable business journalism. We function free from influence by large corporations, political factions, or government bodies. Our editorial decisions are made solely by financial reporters and editors committed to coverage that prioritises the public interest.

Our Mission — Our aim is to provide readers with clear, verifiable reporting on the monetary, commercial, cultural, and technological forces shaping our modern world. We value accuracy, context, and accountability. Our editorial philosophy rests on these core principles:

Accuracy over speed — We prioritise factual correctness above being the first to break a story.

Transparency over spin — We openly disclose our sources, relevant context, and any corrections when errors are identified.

Independence over influence — Our journalism remains free from corporate, political, or governmental manipulation.

Who We Are — Capitalistme is produced by a distributed editorial team of journalists, editors, analysts, and researchers with experience in both digital and traditional media. Our newsroom operates remotely while maintaining consistent editorial standards and oversight, ensuring our coverage remains impartial and free from bias.

Editorial Accountability — Every article is reviewed by editors before publication. Corrections, clarifications, and updates are issued transparently whenever mistakes are found.

Our Promise — Opinion content is clearly labelled and kept separate from news reporting. We never accept payment to advocate for or against coverage. Errors are corrected promptly and openly. Our newsroom operates autonomously, free from conflicting loyalties. Capitalistme is an independent online publication run by a distributed editorial staff based in the United Kingdom.
