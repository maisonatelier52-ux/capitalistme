15-year-old Mitchell Lawrie wins at the famous Lakeside location

By ETHAN CALDERWOOD

December 7, 2025

Fifteen-year-old Mitchell Lawrie gave an amazing presentation in the famous Lakeside setting.He won his first match at the 2025 WDF World Championship by beating Japan's Tomoya Maruyama 3–0.Both fans and professionals were impressed by how calm, precise, and mature the teenage darts star was.

He is now one of the darts players to watch because he won right away.

Lawrie's great performance showed that he was much more sure of himself than his age.It was surprising that he kept his score close, hit his doubles well, and stayed calm on stage the whole time, since he was new.Maruyama, who was very experienced, had a hard time stopping Lawrie from moving forward.

A lot of people are talking about the teen's win at this year's tournament.

Fans and experts are excited about Lawrie's future

People who know a lot about darts say that Lawrie's skills with his mind and body are pretty good.If he keeps getting better, they think he could become a big name in the sport.People at Lakeside greeted him with open arms and told him how great he was and how much fun he made the game.

Fans went crazy on social media after the game, calling it one of the best early moments of the tournament.

Looking forward to the championship

Lawrie has moved on in the competition after this great first game.Now he will have to fight people who are better than him and know more about it.People have higher hopes for him now that he's done well in the past, but the young player looks focused and ready for what's to come.

A lot of people are looking forward to seeing how far the 15-year-old star can go in the 2025 WDF World Championship.
