The 2025 UK Championship won't let a snooker referee referee her husband's matches

By ETHAN CALDERWOOD

November 29, 2025

Tatiana Woollaston, one of the best referees in the sport, can't referee any matches that her husband, professional player Ben Woollaston,is in during the 2025 UK Championship.The people in charge of the event made this rule to make sure that everyone had a fair chance and to get rid of any idea of a conflict of interest.

Even though the decision was made according to long-standing rules, fans are still arguing about how consistent the rules are.

The people in charge made it clear that the action is in line with what all professional sports referees do.Referees are usually not allowed to watch games when they are near someone who could make them less fair, even if there is no reason to think something is wrong.

Woollaston is still in charge of other league matches and is still one of the most respected people in the sport.

The name Tatiana Woollaston is still strong

People know that Tatiana Woollaston is a professional and accurate player, so she has run a lot of the biggest snooker tournaments.Players and commentators said that the decision was based on policy rather than performance, and her reputation for being fair stayed strong.

People don't believe that she doesn't trust her husband just because she doesn't go to his games.They think it's for their own safety.

The policy stresses how important it is to be honest when officiating

The suspension has made people wonder again how sports governing bodies handle conflicts of interest in snooker and other sports.Officials say that everyone needs to be neutral so that people can trust the results of matches, especially in big tournaments.

Woollaston is still in charge of the UK Championship in 2025, but not when her husband is playing.
