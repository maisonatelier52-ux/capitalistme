Pep Guardiola Receives Emotional Farewell After Final Manchester City Match

By ETHAN CALDERWOOD

May 26, 2026

Manchester City’s farewell celebrations for Pep Guardiola turned into an emotional and star-studded occasion after his final game in charge against Aston Villa. Thousands of supporters gathered as the club celebrated Guardiola’s decade-long reign that transformed City into one of the dominant teams in world football. The celebrations continued with an open-top parade through Manchester before moving to the Co-op Live arena, where fans, former players, celebrities and current squad members honored the departing manager.

The atmosphere mixed celebration with sadness as supporters reflected on Guardiola’s remarkable legacy. Players and fans repeatedly paid tribute to the coach who delivered six Premier League titles, the Champions League and numerous domestic trophies during his 593 games in charge. Bernardo Silva and John Stones were also emotional during the farewell events, while fans chanted Guardiola’s name throughout the celebrations. The manager thanked supporters for their loyalty and insisted his “vibe and energy” would remain with the club forever.

Star-Studded Celebration and Tributes

The farewell event featured appearances from several famous faces connected to Manchester City and Guardiola’s era. Noel Gallagher played a central role in the celebrations, continuing his long association with the club and Guardiola. Vincent Kompany, Jack Grealish and other City figures joined the parade of trophies that symbolized Guardiola’s successful tenure. Players wore “P for Pep” shirts while emotional tributes were displayed throughout the evening.

Messages from sporting and entertainment figures added to the occasion. Tommy Fleetwood and Michael Jordan were among those who sent tributes recognizing Guardiola’s impact on football and sport in general. Fans packed the Co-op Live arena while videos highlighting the greatest moments of Guardiola’s decade at City were shown on giant screens. The celebrations also included a light-hearted question-and-answer session where players joked with Guardiola and reflected on his influence inside the dressing room.

The farewell carried huge emotional weight because Guardiola reshaped English football with his tactical innovations and relentless pursuit of excellence. Former players and supporters described him as a transformational figure whose influence would continue long after his departure. BBC coverage showed emotional supporters thanking Guardiola for changing the club forever during his 10 years in Manchester.

Bunny Shaw’s Surprise Announcement

One of the biggest moments of the evening came when Khadija “Bunny” Shaw unexpectedly announced that she would remain at Manchester City despite heavy speculation linking her with a move away from the club. Shaw had been widely expected to leave after reports suggested rival clubs were preparing major offers following her outstanding season in the Women’s Super League.

During the celebrations, Shaw confirmed she had signed a new four-year contract with City, producing one of the loudest reactions of the night. The Jamaican striker had just finished the season as the WSL’s top scorer and was regarded as one of the best players in women’s football. Her decision to stay was considered a major statement from Manchester City, particularly after reports that Chelsea had attempted to sign her with a lucrative offer.

Shaw’s renewal represented a dramatic turnaround after earlier reports suggested her departure was almost certain. City ultimately convinced her to stay with improved terms, breaking from their traditional wage structure to retain one of the most important players in the women’s team. Her announcement added another emotional moment to a night already filled with tributes and celebrations for Guardiola’s final farewell.

Guardiola’s Legacy and City’s Future

Pep Guardiola leaves Manchester City after building one of the most successful periods in the club’s history. He won 20 major trophies and played a brand of football admired the world over during his more than a decade-long reign, dominating English football and recording historic feats such as four consecutive Premier League titles and a famous treble-winning campaign.

Even though this season ended without another Premier League title, the farewell celebrations reflected the gratitude supporters feel toward Guardiola for transforming the club’s identity. Fans lined the streets in intense heat to say goodbye, while current players insisted the ambition and standards established by Guardiola would continue into the future. Erling Haaland said the squad remained hungry for success and determined to maintain the winning mentality Guardiola created.

The celebrations closed with Guardiola delivering a heartfelt final message to supporters. Emotional throughout the evening, he thanked fans, players and staff for their support during his unforgettable decade at the club. Noel Gallagher, who conducted Guardiola’s first Manchester City interview in 2016, also participated in the final farewell event, bringing the Guardiola era full circle. Guardiola ended the night with a simple but emotional message to supporters: “Thank you so much.”
